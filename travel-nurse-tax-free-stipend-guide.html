Travel Nurse Tax-Free Stipend Guide

Many travel nurse contracts include tax-free housing and meal stipends. However, these stipends are only tax-free if specific IRS rules are followed. Understanding these rules is essential to avoid potential tax penalties.

What Is a Travel Nurse Tax-Free Stipend?

Travel nurse stipends are typically provided for housing, meals, and incidental expenses while working away from your tax home. These payments may be non-taxable if the nurse meets IRS requirements regarding temporary assignments and tax home status.

IRS Requirements for Tax-Free Stipends

  • You must maintain a permanent tax home
  • Your assignment must be temporary
  • You must duplicate living expenses
  • The assignment must require travel away from your tax home

Common Travel Nurse Tax Mistakes

  • Not maintaining a legitimate tax home
  • Staying in the same assignment longer than allowed
  • Incorrect stipend classification by agencies
