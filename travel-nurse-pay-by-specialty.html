Travel Nurse Pay by Specialty (2026)

Travel nurse compensation varies significantly depending on specialty demand, hospital staffing shortages, and patient acuity levels. Below are typical weekly pay ranges for common travel nurse specialties across the United States.

Typical Travel Nurse Weekly Pay by Specialty

Specialty Typical Weekly Pay
ICU (Intensive Care) $3,100 – $3,700

Why Specialty Pay Varies

Higher-acuity specialties such as ICU, Cath Lab, and Labor & Delivery typically command higher pay because hospitals experience more severe staffing shortages and require highly experienced nurses. Demand fluctuations can cause weekly pay ranges to vary significantly between contracts.
