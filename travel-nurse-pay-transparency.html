Travel Nurse Pay Transparency (2026)

Many travel nurses struggle to determine whether their contract offers are competitive. Pay transparency helps nurses compare compensation across hospitals, locations, and specialties.

Typical Travel Nurse Weekly Pay Ranges

  • $2,600 – $3,000/week (average contracts)
  • $3,000 – $3,500/week (high-demand regions)
  • $3,500 – $4,000+/week (critical shortage specialties)

Why Pay Transparency Matters

Travel nurse agencies and hospitals often negotiate contracts individually, which means compensation may vary significantly between similar assignments. Transparent salary data helps nurses negotiate stronger contracts and avoid underpaid assignments.
